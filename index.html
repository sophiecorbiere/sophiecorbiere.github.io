Existere's Instagram Campaign

Take a look at an Instagram campaign that I created for Existere, York University's prestigious literary journal. The campaign ran on Instagram from late January 2023 until mid-May 2023 and promoted authors from its most recent issue. I conducted interviews and edited them into short videos for the social media site. I also made graphic posts consisting of biography and quote posts for each author.

Sophie Corbiere: The Greatest!

Link audio file re digital authorship
Since starting university I have learned how to adapt texts to a digital form, and how important form is to content. I have also learned how to write in new genres, such as podcasts, videos and more. I have learnt how to design Instagram posts using Canva. I am most comfortable creating videos; from writing the script, to planning out the shots, to filming and editing them together. However, the most difficult thing I have learnt to do, and thus my biggest accomplishment so far, has been to hand code a website.

While I do consider myself a novice, I am very willing and eager to learn and improve my skills. I hope to expand on these growing skills in this course so that I am more employable when I graduate. I know how important digital authorship is and how mastering this type of communication can help a writer be successful. More cool links.
